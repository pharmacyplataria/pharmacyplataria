Services

Everyday healthcare for the residents and visitors of Plataria, Sivota and Igoumenitsa.

  • Prescriptions

    Electronic, handwritten, private and EU cross-border prescriptions all accepted. In-house compounded (galenic) preparations.

  • Vaccination

    Seasonal flu, travel and booster shots — officially registered.

  • Blood pressure, glucose & cholesterol

    Blood-pressure measurement plus quick capillary-blood glucose & cholesterol tests with dietary advice.

  • First aid

    On-the-spot care for minor injuries — wound cleaning and dressing, help with burns, insect bites and other emergencies.

  • Pharmaceutical advice

    Interaction checks, chronic-therapy guidance, OTC selection — in 4 languages.

  • Baby & child

    Formula, teats, hygiene products and vitamins for the little ones.

  • Cosmetics & parapharma

    Selected dermo-cosmetic and high-protection sunscreen ranges. Wide variety of dietary supplements and orthopaedic shoes.

Your pharmacist

Angeliki Pappa holds a degree in Pharmacy from the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (2000) and has been serving the Plataria community since 2000.

Drawing on years of experience in chronic-disease management and pharmaceutical counselling, we look after both our local community and the visitors of Plataria, Sivota and Igoumenitsa with the same care. The pharmacist and her team focus on impeccable service for every customer — clear instructions, responsible information and personal attention at every visit.

A member of the Thesprotia Pharmaceutical Association and a regular participant in continuing-education programs. Drop in for a chat.

  • Degree in Pharmacy, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki
  • Member, Thesprotia Pharmaceutical Association
  • Team languages: Greek, English, Italian, German
